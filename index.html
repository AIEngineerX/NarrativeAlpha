Methodology

NarrativeAlpha V1 aggregates real-time data with enhanced scam detection:

Data Sources

  • DEX Screener API - Real-time Solana token prices, volume, liquidity, transactions
  • Live Volume Tracking - Platform breakdown across PumpFun, Raydium, Meteora, Orca
  • On-Chain Metrics - Transaction counts, buy/sell ratios, token age

Enhanced Scam Detection (V1)

  • Honeypot Detection - High buy ratio with no price movement (+50 scam score)
  • Slow-Bleed Pattern - High buys but consistent price decline = hidden sell tax (+25 scam score)
  • Sell-Tax Indicator - 75%+ buys with 10%+ price drop (+20 scam score)
  • MC/Liquidity Ratio - >200x ratio auto-filtered, >100x critical warning
  • Tiered PumpFun Filtering - Age-based volume thresholds ($1k-$3k by age)

Confidence Scoring

  • Scam Score 60+ - Confidence reduced by 50 points
  • Scam Score 40+ - Confidence reduced by 35 points
  • Scam Score 20+ - Confidence reduced by 20 points
  • High Risk Override - Positive signals become VERIFY when risk detected

Understanding Signals

Signal Tags

  • PUMPING - Strong upward momentum with volume confirmation
  • FRESH - New launch under 6 hours old
  • NEW PUMP - Fresh launch with immediate price action
  • VERIFY - Price movement needs verification (animated warning)
  • DEAD - No trading activity detected
  • DUMPING - Significant downward price movement

Warning Badges

  • HONEYPOT? - Potential honeypot (sells may be blocked)
  • SLOW BLEED - Hidden sell tax detected
  • HIGH RISK - Multiple risk indicators present
  • SELL TAX - Suspected sell tax from buy/price mismatch
  • DEAD - No trading activity in recent period

DEX Volume Breakdown

  • PumpFun - Pump.fun and PumpSwap volume
  • Raydium - Raydium DEX volume
  • Meteora - Meteora DEX volume
  • Orca - Orca DEX volume

Key Metrics

  • New Tokens - Tokens launched in last 24 hours
  • Avg MCap - Average market cap of tracked tokens
  • Top Gainer - Best performing token by 1h change

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. NarrativeAlpha provides informational signals only. Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research) before making any trading decisions. Memecoins are extremely high risk.

How does scam detection work?

We analyze MC/Liquidity ratios, buy/sell patterns, transaction activity, and price correlation. Tokens scoring above 70 on our scam score are filtered out. Potential honeypots (high buys, no price movement) are flagged with warnings.

Why do some tokens show "DEAD" or "VERIFY"?

DEAD tokens have no recent trading activity (volume under $500/hr, less than 5 transactions). VERIFY tokens show price movement without corresponding volume, which could indicate wash trading or data issues.

How often does data refresh?

Signal feed refreshes every 2 minutes. Trading Activity metrics update every 30 seconds. Ecosystem data (Bonk/Bags) updates every 2 minutes via CoinGecko.

Can I search for specific tokens?

Yes! Use the search bar in the Signals tab. Enter a token symbol, name, or contract address. Click any token card to view detailed charts and analytics on DEX Screener.
