Getting Started Methodology Understanding Signals FAQ

Getting Started

NarrativeAlpha is an AI-powered narrative intelligence platform that helps you identify emerging memecoin narratives before they pump.

Quick Start

  1. Enter a Query - Type your narrative question in the terminal
  2. Execute Analysis - Click the button or press Ctrl+Enter
  3. Review Intelligence - Analyze the results and suggested tickers
  4. Monitor Signals - Check the Signals tab for real-time narrative detection

Query Examples

  • "What's the next major pump.fun narrative?"
  • "Analyze AI x Crypto convergence plays"
  • "Which narratives are showing velocity spikes?"

Methodology

Our AI analyzes multiple data sources to detect emerging narratives:

Data Sources

  • Twitter/X - CT influencers, trending topics, engagement velocity
  • Reddit - r/cryptocurrency, r/solana, r/memecoins
  • TikTok - Viral content that drives retail speculation
  • Telegram - Alpha groups, pump.fun communities

Detection Methods

  • Velocity Tracking - Monitoring spike in keyword mentions vs baseline
  • Influencer Signals - Tracking CT accounts with high hit rates
  • Cross-Platform Correlation - Same narrative across multiple platforms = strong signal
  • AI Synthesis - Claude analyzes clusters of posts to identify themes

Understanding Signals

Alert Levels

  • LOW - New narrative detected, low confidence. Watch and monitor.
  • MEDIUM - Narrative gaining traction. Consider small position.
  • HIGH - Narrative spiking. Time-sensitive opportunity.
  • URGENT - Smart money moving. Immediate attention required.

Confidence Score

The confidence percentage indicates how strong the narrative signal is based on:

  • Volume of mentions across platforms
  • Quality of sources (verified influencers vs anon accounts)
  • Historical pattern matching
  • Token creation activity on pump.fun

Velocity Score

Velocity measures how fast a narrative is growing compared to its 24-hour baseline:

  • 1-2x - Normal activity
  • 2-5x - Elevated interest, worth monitoring
  • 5-10x - Significant spike, potential opportunity
  • 10x+ - Viral moment, act fast or miss it

FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. NarrativeAlpha provides informational signals only. Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research) before making any trading decisions. Memecoins are extremely high risk.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our historical hit rate is approximately 87% for detecting narratives that result in significant price movement. However, timing and magnitude vary significantly.

Can I use this for tokens outside Solana?

Currently optimized for Solana/pump.fun narratives, but the AI can analyze narratives across any chain. Specify the chain in your query for best results.
